The Workflow
REAL WORLD
→ AI GENERATION
1. Export the best viewpoint from your splat as a screenshot
2. Brief Claude — describe the location, angle, lighting, and feeling
3. Ask Claude to write an OpenArt prompt that recreates this angle as a cinematic concept image for your client's film
4. Generate in OpenArt — use the splat screenshot as image reference (image-to-image)
5. Add to your concept library with a note: "inspired by Gaussian splat capture"

Session 3 · 1:00 – 2:30
PLASK.AI
MOTION CAPTURE
90 minutes · Your body becomes animation data
Motion Capture
Every time you've seen an animated character move convincingly — there's a good chance a human performed that movement first.
Motion capture translates human movement into animation data. Today you do it in a browser — for free — using nothing but your phone camera.
How It Works
FOUR STEPS.
ONE BROWSER.
🎬
STEP 1
Record your movement
30–60 seconds. Full body in frame. Good lighting. Plain background helps.
📤
STEP 2
Upload to Plask.ai
Browser-based. No app, no account required. Just upload your video clip.
⏳
STEP 3
Wait 2–5 minutes
Plask analyses frame by frame and extracts the skeleton data from your movement.
🦴
STEP 4
Watch yourself move in 3D
A skeleton that moves exactly as you moved. Exportable to any 3D character.
The Industry
WHERE THIS
SKILL LIVES

Animation Film

Every walking character in a Pixar film has human movement behind it. Motion capture is the invisible performance inside every animated performance.

Video Games

Athletes and stunt performers provide the movement data for game characters. Your favourite game's fluid animation came from a real body.

Cultural Preservation

Indigenous dance forms, traditional craft movements, ceremonial gestures — captured as animation data and preserved for future generations. A movement that might otherwise be lost.

The Question That Matters
What movement from your culture, your family, your identity — is worth preserving?
The movement you're about to capture today will exist as animation data. What would you want that to be?
